Procedure for Simulation

The simulation should be displayed as shown:

  • The circuit simulated is as shown.
  • Click the switches to select the required inputs named "x", "y", "z" and "w". The simulation will display corresponding output.
  • Output is displayed as a glowing bulb
  • At specific positions, small switches make the lines stuck-at-0. Clicking them simulates SA0 faults at the said locations.
  • Repeat above steps for all possible inputs and verify the truth table
